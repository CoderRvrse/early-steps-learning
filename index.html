About Early Steps Learning

We bring quality early childhood education directly to your home, creating a comfortable learning environment where children thrive.

Our Mission

At Early Steps Learning, LLC, we believe the early years are the most critical for brain development. Our mission is to provide research-backed, developmentally appropriate learning experiences that nurture each child's unique potential in the comfort of their own home.

Why In-Home Learning?

  • Children learn best in familiar environments
  • Personalized attention without distractions
  • Flexible scheduling around your family's routine
  • Opportunity for parent involvement and coaching

Our Programs by Age Group

Each program is carefully designed to meet the developmental needs of children at different stages.

Infant Explorers (0-1 year)

Sensory-rich experiences to stimulate early brain development and motor skills.

30-45 minute sessions

Toddler Adventures (1-3 years)

Language development, social skills, and early problem-solving through play.

45-60 minute sessions

Preschool Prep (3-5 years)

Foundational literacy, numeracy, and executive function skills for school readiness.

60-75 minute sessions

School-Age Support (5-8 years)

Homework help, learning reinforcement, and enrichment beyond the classroom.

60-90 minute sessions
